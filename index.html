How It Works

Simple & fast.
Four steps.

Verifying your medicine takes less time than reading the label.

01

Attach the Clip-On

Clip the TMT spectrometer onto your phone's camera. No tools needed — it takes two seconds.

02

Place the Pill

Hold the pill under the sensor head. The app guides you with a live viewfinder.

03

AI Analysis

Near-infrared light captures the chemical fingerprint. Our AI compares it against a verified reference library.

04

Instant Result

Green = Genuine. Red = Counterfeit. Under 10 seconds. Find a trusted pharmacy nearby if needed.
